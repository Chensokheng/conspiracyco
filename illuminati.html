The Illuminati

The Illuminati is a secret society, founded in 1776, with the aim of removing the Church from public life. The modern Illuminati is a powerful and savagely guarded organisation that secretly controls the entire modern world. It has done this through infiltrating the media and seats of power in the world. Politicians, millionaires, royalty and celebrities are some of the members. They control events and brainwash everyone in order to reach their goal - a new world order where a totalitarian group of elites would rule over the whole earth.

JFK's assassination, the push for oil, 9/11 and the appearance of the eye symbol on US bank notes are all attributed to the Illuminati.

Some claim famous celebrities such as Beyonce and Madonna use illuminati imagery such as the triangle and the eye in music videos in order to brainwash the general population.

A 2013 poll of US voters found that 28% believe in the Illuminati, or a society with the same supposed aims.
